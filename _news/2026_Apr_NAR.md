Very happy that our first foray out of the world of proteins and nucleic acids just appeared on [Nucleic Acids Research](https://academic.oup.com/nar/article/54/6/gkag265/8550794). Lipika and Peter develop the first coarse-grained model for poly(ADP-ribose) (a close cousin of RNA that plays important role in DNA damage response).
